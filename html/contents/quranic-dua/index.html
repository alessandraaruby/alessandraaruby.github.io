Dua #1

اَعُوْذُ بِاللهِ اَنْ اَكُوْنَ مِنَ الْجٰهِلِيْنَ

'I seek refuge in Allah from being among Al-Jahilun (the ignorant or the foolish).' (Al-Baqarah:67)

Dua #2

‎رَبَّنَا تَقَبَّلْ مِنَّآ ۖ إِنَّكَ اَنْتَ السَّمِيْعُ الْعَلِيْمُ ۞ وَتُبْ عَلَيْنَآ ۖ إِنَّكَ اَنْتَ التَّوَّابُ الرَّحِيْمُ

'O our Lord! Accept (this service) from us. Verily! You are the All-Hearer, the All-Knower and accept our repentance. Truly, You are the One who Accepts repentance, the Most Merciful.' (Al-Baqarah: 127-128)

Dua #3

‎رَبَّنَآ اٰتِنَا فِىْ الدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةً وَّفِىْ الْاٰخِرَةِ حَسَنَةً وَّقِنَا عَذَابَ النَّارِ ۞

'O our Lord! Give us good in this world, and good in the Hereafter, and save us from the torment of the Fire!' (Al-Baqarah: 201)

Dua #4

‎رَبَّنَآ اَفْرِغْ عَلَيْنَا صَبْرًا وَّثَبِّتْ اَقْدَامَنَا وَانْصُرْنَا عَلَى الْقَوْمِ الْكٰفِرِيْنَ ۞

'O our Lord! Pour forth on us patience, and Set firm our feet and help us (to be victorious) over the disbelieving people.' (Al-Baqarah: 250)

Dua #5

‎رَبَّنَا لَا تُؤَاخِذْنَآ اِنْ نَّسِيْنَآ اَوْ اَخْطَاْنَا رَبَّنَا وَلَا تَحْمِلْ عَلَيْنَآ اِصْرًا كَمَا حَمَلْتَهُۥ عَلَى الَّذِيْنَ مِنْ قَبْلِنَا رَبَّنَا وَلَا تُحَمِّلْنَا مَا لَا طَاقَةَ لَنَا بِهِۦ وَاعْفُ عَنَّا وَاغْفِرْ لَنَا وَارْحَمْنَآ اَنْتَ مَوْلٰٮنَا فَانْصُرْنَا عَلَى الْقَوْمِ الْكٰفِرِيْنَ ۞

'O our Lord! Punish us not if we forget or fall into error, O our Lord! Lay not on us the burden like that which You did lay on those before us. O our Lord! Put not on us the burden greater than we have strength to bear. Pardon us and grant us forgiveness, and have mercy on us. You are our Maula (Supporter, Protector, Patron, Sustainer) so give us victory over the disbelievers.' (Al-Baqarah: 286)

Dua #6

‎رَبَّنَا لَا تُزِغْ قُلُوْبَنَا بَعْدَ إِذْ هَدَيْتَنَا وَهَبْ لَنَا مِن لَّدُنْكَ رَحْمَةً ۚ إِنَّكَ اَنْتَ الْوَهَّابُ ۞

'O our Lord! Let not our hearts deviate (from the truth) after You have Guided us, and Grant us mercy from You. Truly, You are the Bestower.' (Al-Imran: 8)

Dua #7

‎رَبَّنَآ إِنَّنَآ اٰمَنَّا فَاغْفِرْ لَنَا ذُنُوْبَنَا وَقِنَا عَذَابَ النَّارِ ۞

'O our Lord! Indeed we believed, so forgive us our sins and save us from the punishment of the Fire.' (Al-Imran: 16)

Dua #8

‎رَبَّنَآ اٰمَنَّا بِمَآ اَنْزَلْتَ وَاتَّبَعْنَا الرَّسُوْلَ فَاكْتُبْنَا مَعَ الشّٰهِدِيْنَ ۞

'O our Lord! We believe in what You have sent down, and we follow the Messenger, so write us among those who bear witness.' (Al-Imran: 53)

Dua #9

‎رَبَّنَا اغْفِرْ لَنَا ذُنُوْبَنَا وَإِسْرَافَنَا فِىْ اَمْرِنَا وَثَبِّتْ اَقْدَامَنَا وَانْصُرْنَا عَلَى الْقَوْمِ الْكٰفِرِيْنَ ۞

'O our Lord! Forgive us our sins and our transgressions (in keeping our duties), and make firm our feet, and give us victory over the disbelieving people.' (Al-Imran: 147)

Dua #10

‎رَبَّنَا ظَلَمْنَآ اَنْفُسَنَا وَإِنْ لَّمْ تَغْفِرْ لَنَا وَتَرْحَمْنَا لَنَكُوْنَنَّ مِنَ الْخَاسِرِيْنَ ۞

'O our Lord! We have wronged ourselves. And if You Forgive us not, and Bestow not upon us Your Mercy, we shall certainly be from the losers.' (Al-Araf: 23)

Dua #11

‎رَبَّنَآ اَفْرِغْ عَلَيْنَا صَبْرًا وَّتَوَفَّنَا مُسْلِمِيْنَ ۞

«O our Lord! Pour on us patience, and cause us to die as Muslims.» (Al-Araf: 126)

Dua #12

‎حَسْبِىَ اللهُ لَآ إِلٰهَ إِلَّا هُوَ ۖ عَلَيْهِ تَوَكَّلْتُ ۖ وَهُوَ رَبُّ الْعَرْشِ الْعَظِيْمِ ۞

«Allah is sufficient for me. None has the right to be worshiped but He, in Him I put trust and He is the Lord of the Mighty Throne.» (At-Tawbah: 129)

Dua #13

‎رَبَّنَا لَا تَجْعَلْنَا فِتْنَةً لِّلْقَوْمِ الظَّالِمِيْنَ ۞ وَنَجِّنَا بِرَحْمَتِكَ مِنَ الْقَوْمِ الْكٰفِرِيْنَ ۞

«O our Lord! Make us not a trial for the people who are Zalimeen (unjust and wrong doers) (i.e. Do not make them overpower us). And save us by Your Mercy from the disbelieving people.» (Yunus: 85-86)

Dua #14

‎رَبِّ اِنِّىْ اَعُوْذُ بِكَ اَنْ اَسْـَٔلَكَ مَا لَيْسَ لِىْ بِهِۦ عِلْمٌ وَاِلَّا تَغْفِرْ لِىْ وَتَرْحَمْنِىْ اَكُنْ مِّنَ الْخٰسِرِيْنَ ۞

«O my Lord! Indeed I seek refuge in You that I ask You of which I have no knowledge. And if you do not forgive me and have mercy on me, indeed I will be one of the losers.» (Hud: 47)

Dua #15

‎فَاطِرَ السَّمٰوٰتِ وَالْاَرْضِ اَنْتَ وَلِيِّۦ فِىْ الدُّنْيَا وَالْاٰخِرَةِ تَوَفَّنِىْ مُسْلِمًا وَّاَلْحِقْنِىْ بِالصّٰلِحِيْنَ ۞

«The Creator of the Heavens and the Earth! You are my Wali (Protector, Helper, Supporter, Guardian, Lord) in this world and in the Hereafter. Cause me to die as a Muslim (the one submitting to Your Will), and join me with the righteous.» (Yusuf: 101)

Dua #16

‎رَبِّ اجْعَلْنِىْ مُقِيْمَ الصَّلٰوةِ وَمِنْ ذُرِّيَّتِىْ رَبَّنَا وَتَقَبَّلْ دُعَآءِ ۞ رَبَّنَا اغْفِرْ لِىْ وَلِوَالِدَىَّ وَلِلْمُؤْمِنِيْنَ يَوْمَ يَقُومُ الْحِسَابُ ۞

«O my Lord! Make me one of who performs As-Salat (Iqamat-as-Salat), and (also) from my offspring, O our Lord! And accept my invocation. O our Lord! Forgive me and my parents, and (all) the believers on the Day when the reckoning will be established.» (Ibrahim: 40-41)

Dua #17

‎رَبِّ ارْحَمْهُمَا كَمَا رَبَّيَانِىْ صَغِيْرًا ۞

«O my Lord! Bestow on them (my parents) Your Mercy as they brought me up when I was young.» (Al-Isra: 24)

Dua #18

‎رَبِّ اَدْخِلْنِىْ مُدْخَلَ صِدْقٍ وَّ اَخْرِجْنِىْ مُخْرَجَ صِدْقٍ وَّاجْعَلْ لِّىْ مِنْ لَّدُنْكَ سُلْطٰنًا نَّصِيْرًا ۞

«O my Lord! Let my entry be good, and my exit be good. And grant me from You an authority to help me.» (Al-Isra: 80)

Dua #19

‎رَبَّنَآ اٰتِنَا مِنْ لَّدُنْكَ رَحْمَةً وَّهَيِّئْ لَنَا مِنْ اَمْرِنَا رَشَدًا ۞

«O our Lord! Bestow upon us mercy from Yourself, and Facilitate for us our affair in the right way.» (Al-Kahf: 10)

Dua #20

‎رَبِّ اشْرَحْ لِىْ صَدْرِىْ ۞ وَيَسِّرْلِىْ اَمْرِىْ ۞ وَاحْلُلْ عُقْدَةً ۢ مِّنْ لِّسَانِىْ ۞ يَفْقَهُوْا قَوْلِىْ ۞

«O my Lord! Open for me my chest (grant me self-confidence, contentment and boldness). And make my task easy for me. And loosen the knot (the defect) from my tongue. That they may understand my speech.» (Taha: 25-28)

Dua #21

‎رَبِّ زِدْنِىْ عِلْمًا ۞

«O my Lord! Increase me in knowledge.» (Taha: 114)

Dua #22

‎اَنِّىْ مَسَّنِىَ الضُّرُّ وَاَنْتَ اَرْحَمُ الرّٰحِمِيْنَ ۞

«Verily, pain and illness has seized me, and You are the Most Merciful of all those show mercy.» (Al-Anbiya: 83)

Dua #23

‎لَآ اِلٰهَ اِلَّآ اَنْتَ سُبْحٰنَكَ اِنِّىْ كُنْتُ مِنَ الظّٰلِمِيْنَ ۞

«None has the right to be worshipped but You (O Allah) Glory be to You [above all that (evil) they associate with You]! Truly, I have been from the wrongdoers.» (Al-Anbiya: 87)

Dua #24

‎رَبِّ لَا تَذَرْنِىْ فَرْدًا وَّاَنْتَ خَيْرُ الْوٰرِثِيْنَ ۞

«O my Lord! Leave me not single (childless), and You are the Best of the inheritors.» (Al-Anbiya: 89)

Dua #25

‎رَبِّ اَنْزِلْنِىْ مُنْزَلًا مُّبَارَكًا وَّاَنْتَ خَيْرُ الْمُنْزِلِيْنَ ۞

«O my Lord! Cause me to land at a blessed landing place, and You are the best of those who cause to land.» (Al-Mu'minun: 29)

Dua #26

‎رَبِّ اَعُوْذُ بِكَ مِنْ هَمَزٰتِ الشَّيٰطِيْنِ ۞ وَاَعُوْذُ بِكَ رَبِّ اَنْ يَّحْضُرُوْنِ ۞

«O my Lord! I seek refuge with You from the whisperings (suggestions) of the Shayateen (devils). And I seek refuge with You. My Lord! Lest they should come near me.» (Al-Mu'minun: 97-98)

Dua #27

‎رَبِّ اغْفِرْ وَارْحَمْ وَاَنْتَ خَيْرُ الرّٰحِمِيْنَ ۞

«O my Lord! Forgive and (show) mercy, and You are the best of those who show mercy.» (Al-Mu'minun: 118)

Dua #28

‎رَبَّنَا اصْرِفْ عَنَّا عَذَابَ جَهَنَّمَ ۖ إِنَّ عَذَابَهَا كَانَ غَرَامًا ۞ إِنَّهَا سَآءَتْ مُسْتَقَرًّا وَّ مُقَامًا ۞

«O our Lord! Avert from us the torment of Hell. Verily its torment is ever an inseparable, (permanent) punishment. Indeed, it is an evil abode and an evil place to rest (in).» (Al-Furqan: 65-66)

Dua #29

‎رَبَّنَا هَبْ لَنَا مِنْ اَزْوَاجِنَا وَذُرِّيّٰتِنَا قُرَّةَ اَعْيُنٍ وَّاجْعَلْنَا لِلْمُتَّقِيْنَ اِمَامًا ۞

«O our Lord! Bestow on us from our spouses and our offspring the comfort and coolness of our eyes, and make us leaders of the Muttaqeen (the pious).» (Al-Furqan: 74)

Dua #30

‎رَبِّ هَبْ لِىْ حُكْمًا وَّاَلْحِقْنِىْ بِالصّٰلِحِيْنَ ۞ وَاجْعَلْ لِّىْ لِسَانَ صِدْقٍ فِىْ الْآخِرِيْنَ ۞ وَاجْعَلْنِىْ مِنْ وَّرَثَةِ جَنَّةِ النَّعِيْمِ ۞ وَلَا تُخْزِنِىْ يَوْمَ يُبْعَثُوْنَ ۞

«O my Lord! Bestow Hukm (religious knowledge, right judgement of the affairs, power of decision making) on me, and join me with the righteous. And grant me an honorable mention in the later generations. And make me one of the inheritors of the Paradise of Delight. And disgrace me not on the Day when (all creation) will be resurrected.» (Ash-Shuara: 83-85,87)
